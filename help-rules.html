Game Rules

  • Each player given a set budget to spend on athletes.
  • Players must choose 1 athlete/entrant from 100m, 200m, 400m, 800m, 1500m, 5000m, 10000m, 110m hurdles(100m for women), 400m hurdles, 4x100m relay, 4x 400m relay.
  • Athletes must be chosen from both men’s and women’s category, so in effect the player will pick 2 athletes for each event, 1 man and 1 woman.
  • Points are awarded based on athlete’s success in their event, starting right from the heats.
  • Once an event starts the player cannot change their selection for that event.
